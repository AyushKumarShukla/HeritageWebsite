Honours Programme

The Bachelor Of Science In Computer Science is a 3 Year Course Spanning Over 6 Semesters as a Honours Programme Consisting of 4 credits per theory paper and 6 credits per practical paper, accumulating to a total of 10 credit points per subject in any given semester

Course Outline

  • Core Computer Science, Digital Circuitry and Digital Logic, Programming, Computer Architecture, Software Engineering, Networking and Cybersecurity, Web Development along with parallel courses in Machine Learning Using TensorFlow, Artificial Intelligence, Data Science, Big Data, Cloud Computing.
  • Parallel Courses In The Following Subjects (Two subjects to be chosen out of which one spans three odd semesters and the other spans three even semesters)
    • Mathematics
    • Electronics
    • Physics
